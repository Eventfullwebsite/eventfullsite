saturday brunch

coya paris

25 sept. 2021

They provide the perfect setting for your Saturday and Sunday brunch experience! Join them from 12.30pm each week and toast the weekend with a glass of bubbles or Pisco Sour cocktail, as their chefs prepare a selection of delicious brunch dishes including avocado on toast with poached eggs or baked eggs with black beans and spicy tomato with tortillas. Their resident DJ will join them each week playing South-American inspired beats to keep heads nodding, creating an elevated atmosphere, leading you into weekend.
