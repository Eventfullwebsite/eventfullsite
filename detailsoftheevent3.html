showroom: swedish fashion show

institut suédois

29 sept 2021

For one week, Swedish fashion joins Swedish Design Movement and takes over a part of the Swedish Institute with three showrooms, a seminar and meetings. On September 29, 2021, ten Swedish fashion brands - ABOMÉ Studio, AVAVAV, Diemonde, Filippa Agaton, House of Dagmar, Iggy Jeans, Ingy Stockholm, Jeanerica, Remake Stockholm, Susan Szatmàry - will present their latest designs.
