showroom: swedish fashion show

institut suédois

29 sept. 2021

During one week, Swedish fashion joins the Swedish Design Movement and takes over a part of the Swedish Institute with three showrooms, a seminar and meetings. On September the 29th of 2021, ten Swedish fashion brands - ABOMÉ Studio, AVAVAV, Diemonde, Filippa Agaton, House of Dagmar, Iggy Jeans, Ingy Stockholm, Jeanerica, Remake Stockholm, Susan Szatmàry - will present their latest creations.
